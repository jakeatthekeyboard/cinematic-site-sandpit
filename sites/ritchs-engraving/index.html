25 Years of Mastery

A quarter century of marks that last forever

From a workshop on Wale Street in the heart of Bo-Kaap, Ritch has spent over two decades turning raw surfaces into lasting statements. What began as a passion for the burin has grown into Cape Town's most trusted name in both traditional hand engraving and modern laser work.

Every piece — whether a signet ring, a firearm receiver, or a crystal trophy — receives the same unhurried attention. No templates, no shortcuts. Each commission is drawn, discussed, and executed by hand or precision laser to tolerances measured in fractions of a millimetre.
